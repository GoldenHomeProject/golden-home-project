## COSTAR Prompt Framework
Use this for EVERY content generation prompt. Do not skip sections.

- **C**ontext: what niche, what audience, what platform, what moment
- **O**bjective: what action should the viewer take (save, click, buy)
- **S**tyle: voice register (casual friend, authoritative expert, relatable)
- **T**one: emotional register (excited, confessional, surprised)
- **A**udience: demographic + pain point being addressed
- **R**esponse format: exact structure (hook line, body pattern, CTA line)

### Example: Reel script prompt
```
CONTEXT: Instagram Reel for Golden Home Project, home decor on a budget niche, 25-45 yo homeowners/renters, scrolling in the evening.
OBJECTIVE: Drive click to link-in-bio → Mamma Mia Covers affiliate (24-30% commission).
STYLE: Casual friend showing a hack, not a salesperson.
TONE: Confessional transformation ("I was skeptical. I was wrong.")
AUDIENCE: Renters and budget-conscious homeowners who want their space to look expensive.
RESPONSE FORMAT:
- Hook (0-3s): "My [room] looked like this. Same [room]. $[amount]."
- Problem (3-8s): visible ugly thing
- Solution (8-20s): each product on-screen with price overlay
- Reveal (20-28s): wide shot of finished space
- CTA (28-30s): "Link in bio for everything"
```

## Reel Algorithm Rules (2026 Meta ranking)
1. **Watch time is #1** — every second of retention compounds
2. **3-second hook rule** — if you lose them at 3s, the Reel dies
3. **Shares > saves > comments > likes** (in that order for reach)
4. **Original audio** beats trending audio now (algorithm shift 2025→2026)
5. **Native product tagging** boosts conversions 2-3x vs link-in-bio alone
6. **Captions matter for search** — Instagram indexes caption text now
7. **Post at 10 AM and 6 PM ET** for home-decor niche peak engagement
8. **3-5 hashtags** — more than 5 is a spam signal in 2026

## Faceless YouTube Expansion (new revenue lane)
GHP already has 6,700+ YouTube subscribers. The faceless model earns $3K–$15K/month.

### Playbook
1. **Repurpose existing Reels** → 60-second Shorts → stitch 10 into a long-form compilation

3. **AdSense** monetizes Shorts directly (≥4K watch hours + 1K subs = already qualified)
4. **Affiliate links in description** of every long-form video
5. **Target keywords**: "home decor on a budget", "apartment makeover under $100", "amazon finds home"

### Content cadence
- 1 long-form / week (15-20 min compilation)
- 5 Shorts / week (repurposed from long-form + IG Reels)
- Shared description template with all affiliate links
