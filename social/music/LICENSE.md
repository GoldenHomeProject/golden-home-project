## Adding new tracks

1. Confirm license is CC0, CC BY 3.0, CC BY 4.0, or public domain. No CC BY-NC (non-commercial) — we are a commercial LLC.
2. Trim to ~45 seconds with fade in/out at 128 kbps (keeps repo light).
3. Append row to the table above. Always include proper attribution.
4. If switching to CC0 / public domain in the future, attribution line can be dropped — but for now the caption line is MANDATORY under CC BY.
