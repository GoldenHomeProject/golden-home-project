## How to read this

Each cluster has the same 4 dimensions per the queue spec:
1. **Top-3 SERP authority** — who currently owns positions 1-3 for the seed query. High-authority media = harder to break in; niche blogs visible = matchable.
2. **Search-intent classification** — informational / comparison / transactional. Buy-mode only.
3. **Available affiliate offers** — Amazon Associates is the floor; DTC programs noted where they exist publicly. Commission rates marked **VERIFY** if not confirmed against the program's payout page (per the 2026-05-03 unverified-commission rule).
4. **Real anchor product** — one specific SKU/brand with verified Amazon listing that GHP could review as a pillar.

A cluster is **viable** if (a) at least one top-3 result is a niche blog (not Wirecutter / Consumer Reports / Home Depot), AND (b) the affiliate offer exists and the anchor SKU is real.
